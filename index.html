I also spent a big part of my time dealing with client and vendor relations. There I worked on re-vamping pre-built e-commerce websites on a platform called AmeriCommerce. As well, I built new websites for clients using WordPress. After several months, I realized I wanted to hone my developer skills even more and looked into coding bootcamps. I found as bootcamp called Epicodus that seemed like the right fit as the school showed amazing potential to help my skills grow.
